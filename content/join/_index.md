# Join DS@GT ARC

If you want to get involved with DS@GT Applied Research Competitions, start here.

Most new members begin with the [Fall 2025 Interest Group](/join/fall-2025/), which is designed to help students build the background needed for ARC's spring competition teams. Returning members and qualified students can then continue into the [Spring 2026 CLEF Competition](/join/spring-2026/).

## Best path for most students

1. Read the [Fall 2025 Interest Group](/join/fall-2025/) page.
2. Review the [FAQ](/faq/) to understand expectations, roles, and time commitment.
3. Visit [Contact](/contact-us/) if you still have questions.
4. Explore [Impact](/impact/) and [Publications](/publications/) to see what ARC teams produce.

## Current opportunities

- [Fall 2025 Interest Group](/join/fall-2025/) — the main onboarding path for new members.
- [Spring 2026 CLEF Competition](/join/spring-2026/) — for returning members and qualified students preparing to compete and publish.
